Frequently asked questions

What is Cascade Digital Marketing?

Cascade Digital Marketing is a platform designed to help businesses manage and post content efficiently across multiple social and paid platforms. It acts as your own digital marketing co-pilot.

What Marketing Platforms Does Cascade Support?

Cascade covers three main digital marketing segments:

  • Always on brand presence including Google Merchant Centre, Business Profiles, Maps, Facebook Page Details, and Bing Places for Business.
  • Social Media through Facebook, Instagram, X, LinkedIn, Reddit, Pinterest, Tiktok, Youtube, and more.
  • Paid advertising through Google Search and Display as well as promotions on social media.
Can I customize my posts?

Yes. Cascade will suggest content and you can tailor your content as much as you would like to.

What features does Cascade Digital Marketing offer?

Our platform offers features such as automated content scheduling, multi-platform posting, analytics tracking, and integration with various social media and advertising channels.

How much does Cascade cost?

Setup your brand on the house. We will sync it to the main business endpoints for free.  If you decide to produce ongoing content there is a monthly cost and volume cap. Paid marketing is billed as a percentage of total spend.

What if I dont like the proposed strategy?

You are always in control. Any promotion or created content is always visible ahead of time and editable by you. Our automated systems will learn over time what you changed and do better in the future.

What kind of analytics does Cascade Digital Marketing provide?

We offer detailed analytics on engagement, reach, and performance across all connected platforms to help you measure the success of your campaigns.

How is Payment Done?

Payment is done via credit card

How do I know if the tool is working?

There is a feed log of all content that is posted that will be updated as each new post is made. You are able to see and connect to all posts.
